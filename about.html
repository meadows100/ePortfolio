Welcome

Hi, I'm Richard. I live in Colchester which is in Essex, over in the eastern part of the UK. I have lived here most of my life but have travelled much of the world. I am very sporty and especially enjoy football and golf. I love the outdoors and like to go hiking, running and cycling whenever I can. I have successfully climbed Mont Blanc, I completed the "Three Peaks" and I recently took part in the 2021 London Marathon which I must say was one of the best days of my life. My next adventure I am planning is to have a go at climbing Mount Kilimanjaro!

This site has been created to showcase my learning journey throughout my Msc Cyber Security course at the University of Essex Online. There is a different page for each of the modules, and I hope you enjoy the journey as much as I hope to!

I have worked in the IT sector for the best part of the last two decades. I have enjoyed it, I consider it a good career choice, and I am super excited to now be moving in to specialising in the Cybver Security field. I feel overwhelmed and excited in equal measures to be starting on this course with all of my peers and wish us all the best of luck.
